  • International Statitical Classification of Dieases and related health problems - 10th revision Australian Modification is a modified version of orginal ICD-10. It was introduced into Australia in 1998 and been adopted by other countries, such as New Zealand,Ireland and Saudi Arabia.
  • ICD-10 involves 22 chapters and cover including infection, parasitic, neoplasms and etc. Detialed info click me.
  • ICD-10 is originaly developed by WHO. It is a medical classification list on diease, symptoms, signs, abnormals, source of injury and phenotypes by code system. It containing over 155,000 of unique codes to represent such terms in the lastest version.
  • ICD-10-AM is a slightly modified version of ICD-10 by the National Centre for Classification in Health at the University of Sydney and currently maintained by the Australian Consortium for Classification Development.
  • ICD-10(including different national adoptions) give healthcare provider ability to decribe patients symptoms or dieases in a same method which eliminate the confusion and potential misunderstanding
  • Fan fact: ICD-11 is released at 2018 which is even more comprehensive than ICD-10.
